PORK ADOBO RECIPE

PORK ADOBO

Malinamnam na adobong baboy na hindi matatanggihan.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 kg (2.2 lbs) pork belly, cut into chunks
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2-3 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar (optional, for a hint of sweetness)
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil

PREPARATIONS

  1. Marinate the pork:
    2.
    • In a bowl, combine pork, soy sauce, garlic, and bay leaves.
    • Let it marinate for 30 minutes to 1 hour (or overnight in the fridge for deeper flavor).
  2. Sear the pork (optional but recommended):
    3.
    • Heat oil in a pan.
    • Remove pork from marinade (reserve marinade) and sear until browned on all sides.
    • This step adds extra flavor and texture.
  3. Simmer the adobo:
    4.
    • Add the reserved marinade back to the pan.
    • Pour in vinegar and water.
    • Add peppercorns and bring to a boil without stirring (to prevent the vinegar from tasting raw).
    • Once it boils, reduce heat to low and simmer for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until pork is tender.
  4. Finish and reduce:
    5.
    • Taste and adjust seasoning with salt or sugar if needed.
    • If you want a thicker sauce, continue to simmer uncovered until the sauce reduces to your liking.
  5. Serve:
    6.
    • Best served hot with steamed rice!
