PORK SINIGANG

Tender pork and veggies na lutong bahay, you gotta try this if you're into Pinoy Flavors.

PORK SINIGANG RECIPE

INGREDIENTS

  • Pork Belly
  • Sinigang Mix tamarind
  • Taro (Gabi)
  • Radish (Labanos)
  • String beans (Sitaw)
  • Eggplant (Talong)
  • Kangkong leaves
  • Long green chili peppers (Siling haba)
  • Fish sauce (Patis)
  • water
  • Ground black pepper (Optional)

PREPARATIONS

  1. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Sauté the onions until they start to separate into layers.
  2. Add half of the tomatoes and continue sautéing for about 2 minutes.
  3. Stir in the pork belly. Cook while stirring occasionally until the meat begins to brown, then add approximately 1 tablespoon of fish sauce.
  4. Pour in enough water to cover the ingredients. Cover the pot and bring it to a boil.
  5. Once boiling, add the Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc with Gabi mix. Cover again and adjust the heat to low or medium. Simmer for about 30 minutes.
  6. Add the labanos (radish). Cover the pot and continue cooking for another 30 minutes.
  7. Add the eggplant, string beans, and okra. Let cook for 5 minutes.
  8. Mix in the kangkong stalks and the remaining tomatoes. Cook for 3 more minutes.
  9. Adjust the seasoning with fish sauce and ground black pepper to taste.
  10. Finally, add the kangkong leaves. Turn off the heat, cover the pot, and let it sit for 2 minutes before serving.
  11. Transfer to a serving bowl and enjoy hot.
